## 3. 求导运算法则

$$[\lambda f(x) \pm \mu g(x)]' = \lambda f'(x) \pm \mu g'(x) \tag{1}$$

$$
\begin{aligned}
    [f(x) g(x)]' & = f'(x) g(x) + f(x) g'(x) \\
    [f(x) g(x) h(x)]' & = f'(x) g(x) h(x) + f(x) g'(x) h(x) + f(x) g(x) h'(x)
    \tag{2}
\end{aligned}
$$

$$[\frac{f(x)}{g(x)}]' = \frac{f'(x) g(x) - f(x) g'(x)}{[g(x)]^2}$$
