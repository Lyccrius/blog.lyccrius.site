## 1. 指数运算

### $a^x$ 法则

$$
\begin{aligned}
    a^{x + y} & = a^x \cdot a^y \\
    a^{x - y} & = a^x / a^y \\
    (a^x)^y & = a^{xy}
\end{aligned}
$$
