## 1. 平均变化率 瞬时变化率

$$
\begin{aligned}
    \frac{\Delta y}{\Delta x} & = \frac{f(x_2) - f(x_1)}{x_2 - x_1} \\
    & = \frac{f(x_0 + \Delta x) - f(x_0)}{\Delta x}
\end{aligned}
$$

## 2. 基本初等函数导数

$$
\begin{aligned}
    (C)' & = 0 \\
    (a^x)' & = a^x \ln a \\
    (e^x)' & = e^x \\
    (\log_a x)' & = \frac{1}{x \ln a} \\
    (\ln x)' & = \frac{1}{x} \\
    (x^b)' & = b x^{b - 1} \\
    (x^2)' & = 2 x \\
    (x^3)' & = 3 x^2 \\
    (x^{\frac{1}{2}})' & = \frac{1}{2} x^{-\frac{2}{2}} \\
    (x)' & = 1 \\
    (\sin x)' & = \cos x \\
    (\cos x)' & = - \sin x
\end{aligned}
$$
